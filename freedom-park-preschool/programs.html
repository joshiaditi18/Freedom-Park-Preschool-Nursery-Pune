Learning Journeys

Each program is thoughtfully crafted to nurture your child's unique potential

🍼

Playgroup

1.5 – 2.5 years

Our youngest learners are introduced to a warm, caring environment through sensory play, music, rhymes and stories. The focus is on social bonding, basic motor skills and joyful exploration.

🎵 Rhymes 🎨 Sensory Play 🐘 Stories 🤝 Social Skills
🌱

Nursery

2.5 – 3.5 years

Children begin structured learning through colorful activities, early literacy, number awareness and creative arts. We focus on language development, curiosity and independence.

🔤 ABCs 🔢 Numbers 🎨 Craft 🗣️ Language
🌟

Junior KG

3.5 – 4.5 years

Structured curriculum with phonics, writing practice, math concepts and science exploration. Children develop problem-solving skills and a love for learning through hands-on activities.

📝 Writing 🔬 Science 🧮 Maths 🎭 Phonics
🎓

Senior KG

4.5 – 6 years

School-readiness program focusing on reading, writing, arithmetic and social confidence. Children are prepared to transition smoothly to primary school with strong foundational skills.

📖 Reading 🎤 Confidence 🏫 School Prep 🌏 GK
🏠

Daycare

1.5 – 6 years

A safe, nurturing second home for your child while you're at work. We provide healthy meals, nap time, supervised play and age-appropriate activities in a hygienic and loving environment.

🍱 Meals 😴 Nap Time 🛡️ Safe 🎮 Activities
